# Product Philosophy

Seasons exists to help people build lasting financial achievements through one intentional weekly habit.

Most finance apps begin by asking for data. Seasons begins by asking what the user is trying to build.

## The Four Seasons

### Establish
Build your financial foundation.

### Grow
Increase your financial strength.

### Steward
Use your resources with intention.

### Preserve
Protect your financial independence.

These are not levels. They are periods of focus.

## The Weekly Practice

Observe. Reflect. Progress.

The weekly review is not just data entry. It is a moment of attention that helps the user notice what changed and decide what deserves focus.

## The Promise

Your season may change. Your weekly habit remains the same.
